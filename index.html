About

"Good practice travels. This platform ensures that mine does."

Twenty-four years in the classroom represent more than experience; they reflect sustained engagement with evolving curricula, advancing technologies, and generations of learners who expect clarity, relevance, and purpose in what they are taught.

My professional work spans IB PYP, Cambridge, and ICSE, with a consistent focus on integrating technology to strengthen thinking, inquiry, and application in meaningful ways.

This space brings together classroom practice, lesson design, and considered reflection on ICT, AI, and digital learning, shared with educators who approach their craft with intent and rigour.

At its core, the work is committed to structured, student-centered learning, where technology is not just an addition, but an integral part of how understanding is developed and demonstrated.
